<h1>How to Log In (Student)</h1>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>You can log in to SLS in 2 ways:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Through MIMS or</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Through the existing SLS login page.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/4Troubleshooting/Login_2ways.png">
</div>
<h2>Log in with MIMS (for Secondary and JC/CI students)</h2>
<hr>
<p>This option is only applicable to MIMS users. For users whose SLS username
begins with SLS-XXX, please log in via SLS existing login page.</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From the main login page, click&nbsp;<strong>Login With MIMS</strong>.
Next, select <strong>Login for Teaching &amp; Learning</strong>. For eSBA,
select <strong>Login for e-Assessment</strong>.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/4Troubleshooting/Login_MIMS.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>At the MIMS login page, enter your MIMS username and password.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/4Troubleshooting/LT_MIMSHomepage.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Sign In</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Upon successful authentication, you will be brought to the SLS homepage.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Unable to Sign-in to MIMS Account</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Please approach your teacher or MIMS student administrator for assistance.
If you are unable to contact a teacher but urgently need to access SLS,
you can click <strong>Login With SLS</strong> on the main login page to log
in with your SLS username and password.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Log in via SLS Existing Login Page (for Primary students)</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From the main login page, click&nbsp;<strong>Login With SLS</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter your SLS username and the full MIMS email domain name will appear
automatically in the dropdown list for selection.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Key in your SLS password.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Login for Teaching and Learning</strong>. For eSBA, select <strong>Login for e-Assessment</strong>.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/4Troubleshooting/Login_SLSUsername.png">
</div>
<p><u>Note</u>: From R4 (Jun 2026), Primary 1 students will be able to log
in to SLS via MIMS directly, without needing to use their SLS credentials
to activate their accounts. This also means that they do not need to set
up an SLS password if MIMS is their preferred login method.</p>
</li>
</ol>
