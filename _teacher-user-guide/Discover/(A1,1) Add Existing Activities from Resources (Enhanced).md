<h1 id="add-existing-activities-from-resources">(A1,1) Add Existing Activities from Resources (Enhanced)</h1><hr>
<h2 id="from-create-edit-lesson">From Create/Edit Lesson</h2>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>From the homepage, click <strong>Create/Edit Module</strong>. Alternatively, from My Drive page, click <strong>Create/Edit</strong> followed by <strong>New Module</strong>.</li>
<li>By default, the <strong>Create New</strong> tab will be shown. In the <strong>Create/Edit Module</strong> subpage, click <strong>Add Existing</strong> and select between <strong>From Library</strong> and <strong>From Starred Resources</strong>.</li>
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_Edit_New_1.png"></p>
<li>You may search for Modules <a target="_blank" href="/teacher-user-guide/discover/search-for-resources/">across MOE Library, Community Gallery and My Drive</a>. You can also search for Modules from Starred Resources. You can also apply Search Filter to narrow down your search. Search for Module by entering keywords from their titles.</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Module Card</strong> or <strong>View Details</strong> icon to view the Module Details.</li>
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 50%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_Edit_New_2.png"></p>
<li>Select the Activities you wish to add to your new Module by selecting the relevant checkboxes.</li>
<li>To view the existing Module in a new tab, click the <strong>Expand</strong> icon <img style="width:1.2rem; display: inline;" src="/images/Icons/external-link.svg">.</li>
<li>To add your selected Activities, click <strong>Choose</strong>.</li>
<li>The chosen Activities will inherit the Activity Details from the Module in Library or Starred Resources. To view and edit the Activity Notes and Active Learning Process in the <strong>Activity Details</strong> Card, click the <strong>Settings</strong> icon on the <strong>Activity Details</strong> Card. These fields are optional and will not be visible to students.</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="from-module-editor">From Module Editor</h2>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>In the Module Editor, there are multiple ways to add an Activity:
<ul>
<li>Click the <strong>Expand Activity List</strong> icon <img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" src="/images/Icons/ActivityListExpand.svg"> to open the Full Activity List. Select <strong>+ New</strong>.
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_AddNew_1.png"></p>
</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Expand Activity List</strong> icon to open the Full Activity List. Click the <strong>Overflow</strong> (<strong>3-dot</strong> icon) found to the right of any Activity/Section/Module Cover and click <strong>Add New</strong>.
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/create_addnew_22.png"></p>
</li>
<li>From the Collapsed Activity List, click <strong>+</strong>.
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_AddNew_3.png"></p>
</li>
<li>From the Module Plan, click <strong>Add New</strong>.
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/D-AddNew1.png"></p>
</li>
<li>From any Activity, click <strong>Add New</strong>.
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/D-AddNew3.png"></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="add-activities-from-library">Add Activities from Library</h2>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>From the Add New subpage, click on the <strong>Overflow</strong> (<strong>3-dot</strong> icon) next to the Activity and select <strong>Add From Library</strong>.</li>
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_AddLibrary_1.png"></p>
<li>You may search for Activities <a target="_blank" href="/teacher-user-guide/discover/search-for-resources/">across MOE Library, Community Gallery and My Drive</a>. You can also apply Search Filter to narrow down your search.</li>
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_AddLibrary_2.png"></p>
<li>Select the Activities you wish to add to your new Module by selecting the relevant checkboxes.</li>
<li>To view the existing Module in a new tab, click the <strong>Expand</strong> icon <img style="width:1.2rem; display: inline;" src="/images/Icons/external-link.svg">.</li>
<li>To add your selected Activities, click <strong>Choose</strong>.</li>
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_AddLibrary_3.png"></p>
<li>The chosen Activities will inherit the Activity Details from the Module in Library or Starred Resources. To view and edit the Activity Notes and Active Learning Process in the <strong>Activity Details</strong> Card, click the <strong>Settings</strong> icon on the <strong>Activity Details</strong> Card. These fields are optional and will not be visible to students.</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="add-activities-from-starred-resources">Add Activities from Starred Resources</h2>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>From the Add New subpage, select <strong>Add From Starred Resources</strong>.</li>
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_AddLibrary_4.png"></p>
<li>You may search for modules from your Starred Resources by entering keywords from their titles.</li>
<li>Click the <strong>Module Card</strong> or <strong>View Details</strong> icon to view the Module Details.</li>
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_AddStarredResources_2.png"></p>
<li>Select the Activities to add into the Module and select <strong>Choose</strong>.</li>
<p><img alt="Add Existing Activities from Resources" style="width: 100%;" src="/images/2Teacher/Create_AddStarredResources_3.png"></p>
</ol>
