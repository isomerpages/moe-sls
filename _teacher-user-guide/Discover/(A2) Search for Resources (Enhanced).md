<h2>Search for Resources</h2>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From the <strong>MOE Library</strong>, <strong>Community Gallery</strong>, <strong>Find Resources</strong> page,
or when using <strong>Add Modules from Search</strong>, click on the <strong>Search Bar</strong> at
the top of the page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter your search term(s) in the Search Bar. You may search by resource
title, subject, level, content map, or keywords.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Press <strong>Enter</strong> to view the Search Results page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Browse resources using the Search display and select the relevant resource
tab, such as <strong>Modules</strong>, <strong>Questions</strong>, or <strong>Media</strong>,
where applicable.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use the available filters, such as <strong>Level</strong> and <strong>Subject</strong>,
to refine your search results.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select filters as needed. The results count will refresh automatically
in real time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on a resource card to view more details, like lmodule descriptions,
subject tags, etc, that are aligned to your desired learning outcomes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the <strong>Expand icon</strong> to open a resource in a new tab.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use the <strong>Overflow (3-dot) icon</strong> for additional actions, where
available.</p>
</li>
</ol>
