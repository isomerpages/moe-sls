<h2>Personalise SLS Homepage (New)</h2>
<p></p>
<h3>About SLS Homepage</h3>
<p>Students can personalise their SLS dashboard according to their learning
needs by pinning, unpinning, and rearranging frequently used shortcuts,
allowing quick and easy access.</p>
<p></p>
<h3>Navigate the SLS Homepage</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Upon login, <strong>Groups</strong>, <strong>Assignments</strong>, <strong>Announcements</strong>,
and <strong>Planner</strong> will be displayed on the homepage. Under Groups,
you will see either your pinned or most recent class groups. For Assignments,
Announcements, and Planner, the most recent items will be displayed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The three most recently accessed resources will appear under ‘<strong>Continue where you left off</strong>’
for easy access. Click <strong>View All</strong> to see more items.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2Teacher/Homepage_1.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>Open the left menu to view your pinned shortcuts. Click <strong>View Menu</strong> to
see all shortcuts in SLS. New or enhanced tools are labelled, and each
shortcut can be clicked to go directly to its page.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/1Student/homepage.jpg">
</div>
<p>Note: To return to the previous SLS homepage version, select <strong>Switch to Classic Homepage</strong> at
the top right-hand corner.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
<h3>Personalise your SLS Dashboard</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Open the left menu and click <strong>Edit Shortcuts</strong> to personalize
your SLS dashboard based on your learning needs and usage.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select shortcuts by pinning and/or unpinning them. You can pin up to 10
shortcuts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the <strong>Pinned Shortcuts</strong> menu, rearrange the shortcuts using
drag-and-drop.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Save</strong> when done.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/1Student/homepage_shortcuts.png">
</div>
</li>
</ol>
<p></p>
<h3>Navigate using Screen Reader</h3>
<p>Press <strong>Tab</strong> to move through the interface with your keyboard.
Navigation begins at the top taskbar, moving from left to right across
the icons, then continues to the icons in the left menu, and finally to
the main page. Press <strong>Tab</strong> again to move to the next icon.
<br>
</p>
