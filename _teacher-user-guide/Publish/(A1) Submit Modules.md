<h1>(A1) Submit Modules</h1>
<hr>
<p>If you have a Module that you would like to share with teachers on SLS,
you may submit it to the Community Gallery.</p>
<h2>Module Submission Checklist:</h2>
<hr>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Update the Module Description to give other users an overview of what
the Module is about.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Tag your Module with the appropriate Module Tag. You may refer to <a href="/teacher-user-guide/author/add-module-tags/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Add Module Tags</a> for
more information.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure collaborative features (eg. Padlet, Mentimeter, Nearpod, etc.)
and documents (e.g. Google Docs, Google Slides, etc.) have read-only rights
because embedded content can be viewed by all.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Ensure your Module meets <a href="/teacher-user-guide/publish/copyright-guidelines-for-submission/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Copyright guidelines</a>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Your submission will be featured after it has been approved by one of
the Community Gallery Administrators (CG Admins).</p>
<h2>Check Module for Submission</h2>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Open the Module that you wish to submit to the Community Gallery.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the <strong>Overflow</strong> (<strong>3-dot</strong> icon) on the
top right of the header, and select <strong>Check for Submission (Community Gallery)</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You will be prompted to check that the content of the Module is in line
with the <a href="/teacher-user-guide/publish/copyright-guidelines-for-submission/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Copyright guidelines</a>.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Submit Modules" src="/images/2Teacher/P-CG.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>If the <strong>Module is ready for submission</strong>, check the box to
acknowledge that the content complies with SLS Terms of Use and the Copyright
Guidelines.</p>
<p><u>Note</u>: To make any edits before submission, close the Community
Gallery Submission subpage and click the <strong>Pencil</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Pencil.svg">
</div>
<p>to enter the Module Editor page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>On the same subpage, you may wish to rename the Module Title, and edit
the Permission Settings by selecting the checkboxes beside:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Allow teachers to copy/assign/publish and students to copy this Module</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Allow teachers and students to view print-friendly worksheet</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Allow students to reattempt Module as self-study</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>Once done, click <strong>Submit</strong> to proceed. After submitting to
Community Gallery, a notification message will indicate that you have submitted
a copy of your Module for approval.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>After the module has been submitted, a shareable link will be generated
(see image below). This link can be distributed to teachers, allowing them
to access and copy the module for use in their schools.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Submitted_Module_Link.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>You will receive a notification informing you whether the the Community
Gallery Administrators have approved your submission or returned it.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Resubmit Modules</h2>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>
<p>You will receive a notification if your module has been returned.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 50%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Submit Modules" src="/images/2Teacher/P-CG1.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the notification to open the copy of the Module, which will show
a banner asking you to please check feedback.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may either click on Feedback or Admin Reviews to open the Admin Reviews
subpage which will inform you of the reason(s) why your Module has been
returned.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may respond to the administrator by clicking on Add Review.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To resubmit your Module to the Community Gallery after making the edits,
click the <strong>Overflow</strong> (<strong>3-dot</strong> icon) on the top
right of the header, and click <strong>Check for Submission (Community Gallery)</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
