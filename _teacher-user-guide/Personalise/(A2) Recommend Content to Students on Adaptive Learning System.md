<h1>(A2) Recommend Content to Students on Adaptive Learning System</h1>
<hr>
<p>To provide differentiated guidance to students, you can recommend topic(s)/ subtopic(s) in ALS for selected students, who will receive a notification and a link to access the recommended content in ALS.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Select the students in the class group by clicking on the checkboxes beside their names.</p>
<img alt="Recommend Content to Students on Adaptive Learning System" style="width: 80%;" src="/images/2Teacher/PE_RecommendContent.png">
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Recommend Topics.</strong></p>
<img alt="Recommend Content to Students on Adaptive Learning System" style="width: 80%;" src="/images/2Teacher/PE_RecommendContent1.png">
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the number of weeks the recommended topic(s)/ subtopic(s) will be prioritised.</p>
<img alt="Recommend Content to Students on Adaptive Learning System" style="width: 80%;" src="/images/2Teacher/PE_RecommendContent2.png">
</li>
	<u>Note</u>:
<ul>
<li>Teachers can set the number of weeks the recommendation will be active in ALS.</li>
<li>The default duration is 1 week and can be adjusted up to 3 weeks.</li>
</ul>
<li>
<p>Select topic(s)/ subtopic(s) to recommend.</p>
<img alt="Recommend Content to Students on Adaptive Learning System" style="width: 80%;" src="/images/2Teacher/PE_RecommendContent3.png">
</li>
<li>
<p>Access Recommended ALS Topics by <a target="_blank" href="/teacher-user-guide/notify/view-and-create-announcements/">viewing Notifications</a>.</p>
</li>
<p><u>Note</u>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Students will be alerted to their teacherâ€™s recommendation via a notification.</li>
<li>Each recommended topic or subtopic will have its own notification.</li>
<li>Clicking on the notification will bring students into ALS, where they will be prompted to select a learning mode. The recommended learning mode will be determined by ALS engine.</li>
</ul>
</ol>
