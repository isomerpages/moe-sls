<h1>(A1,v) Add or Sort Assignments in Group</h1>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>
<p>You can add assignments to the Group from the Group page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Access Groups from the left menu or by selecting the Group (if pinned)
on your homepage. On the Group page, click <strong>Add Assignment</strong> to
add the assignment:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From New Module</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From My Drive</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From Library</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From Starred Resources</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may also add a new Category to your Group assignment. This can be
used to organise the Assignments for your Group.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the <strong>Overflow</strong> (<strong>3-dot</strong> icon) on the
Assignment card to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.learning.moe.edu.sg/teacher-user-guide/assess/monitor-students-responses-in-an-assignment/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Monitor Assignment</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>View details of the Assignment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.learning.moe.edu.sg/teacher-user-guide/assign/manage-assignments/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Manage Assignment</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Move the Assignment to another category</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Uncategorise the Assignment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="https://www.learning.moe.edu.sg/teacher-user-guide/assign/move-assignments/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Move the Assignment to another Group</a>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Add Assignments to Group" src="/images/2Teacher/AS_tempmsg.png">
</div>
<p><u>Note</u>: A temporary message will appear when the Assignment has been
successfully:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Moved to another category</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Uncategorised</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Moved to another Group</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Sort Assignments in Group</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>You can sort Assignments in Groups to find them easily.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>On the Assignment tab in the group, select a category from the dropdown
menu. Click <strong>Apply</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Sort By</strong> and you can now sort the Assignments according
to Assignment title, due or start date.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Sort Assignments in Class Group" src="/images/2Teacher/as_addassignment3.jpg">
</div>
<h2>Sort Resources in Group</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>You can sort Resources in Groups to find them easily.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>On the Resource tab in the Group, select a category from the dropdown
menu. Click <strong>Apply</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Sort By</strong> and you can now sort the Resources according
to modified date, date added or title.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Sort Resources in Class Group" src="/images/2Teacher/as_addassignment2.jpg">
</div>
<h2>Access Resources and Assignments for Newly Added Students</h2>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>
<p>From June 2026 onwards, access to multiple Group assignments can be granted
at the same time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students who are newly added to the Group will automatically have access
to the Group Resources.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To grant access to assignments, Group Owners and Co-teachers can assign
one or more assignments to the new student.</p>
<p>a. Click <strong>Assign to Student</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/images_2Teacher_as_addassignmenttonewstudents.png">
</div>
<p>b. Select the new student and click <strong>Next</strong>.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/images_2Teacher_as_addassignmenttonewstudents2.png">
</div>

process</p>
