<h1>(A3,7) Insert Drawings</h1>
<hr>
<p>In the Rich Text Editor, click the <strong>Drawing</strong> icon (Palette).</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>By default, after clicking the <strong>Drawing</strong> icon, you will start
with a blank canvas.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can click on the <strong>Image button</strong> to upload an image as
background from your computer.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can resize the images you have uploaded by dragging the points on
the corners of the image.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the top bar, select objects on the canvas, such as drawings, images
or text, to <strong>Move</strong>, <strong>Delete</strong>, <strong>Undo</strong> or <strong>Redo</strong> actions,
or arrange them in layers by bringing them <strong>Forward</strong> or sending
them <strong>Backward</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>By default, the image will be fit to height within the canvas. Click on <strong>Fit to Width</strong> or
use <strong>Zoom</strong> to adjust how the image fills the screen.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can use the eraser to erase any part of the drawing. You can also
adjust the thickness of the pen, highlighter and eraser.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>When you click <strong>Clear All</strong>, all drawings and images will
be deleted.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can resize/rotate the final drawings/images before inserting them
into the textbox.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can <strong>Star</strong> the final drawing as a Resource or <strong>Download</strong> it
onto your computer.</p>

</li>
</ul>
<h2>Drawing Tools</h2>
<hr>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Pen, highlighter and eraser</strong>: Choose from 5 tip sizes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Colours</strong>: Choose from 8 colours by clicking on the colour
palette.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To draw with a mouse, click and drag the cursor using your mouse.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To draw with your finger or stylus on a tablet, apply pressure on the
screen while moving your finger or stylus.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Text</strong>: To insert text, click on the text box button and
begin typing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Image</strong>: To insert a background image, click on the image
button and upload an image from your computer.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Draw/write on the background image using the pencil tool or by inserting
a text box.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Insert a text over the background image using the text box.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Delete</strong>: To erase any part of your drawing or text, click
the <strong>Select</strong> icon, select the part and click&nbsp;<strong>Delete</strong>.
You can also use the eraser to erase your drawing or text.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Move:</strong> To move your drawing or text, click the <strong>Select</strong> icon,
select the object or text and move it.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Undo/Redo:</strong> Undo/redo the most recent stroke by clicking&nbsp;<strong>Undo/Redo</strong>. <strong><s>(NEW)</s></strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Clear All:</strong> Click <strong>Clear All</strong> to remove all
drawings and images, including the original background image. If this was
done in error, click <strong>Undo</strong> to restore your work.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on <strong>Fit to Width</strong> or use Zoom to adjust how the drawing
fills the screen.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Scroll:</strong> Use two fingers on your trackpad or tablet to
scroll up and down image.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2>Resizing/Rotating the Final Drawing</h2>
<hr>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Click&nbsp;<strong>Next</strong>&nbsp;after you are done drawing/editing.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Rotate or crop the image to your preferred dimensions before clicking <strong>Done</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h2>Edit uploaded drawing</h2>
<hr>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To edit the drawing, click the&nbsp;<strong>Edit Image</strong>&nbsp;icon
to open up the drawing tool again.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To delete the entire drawing, click the&nbsp;<strong>Delete</strong>&nbsp;icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To download the entire drawing, click the <strong>Download</strong> icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the&nbsp;<strong>Settings</strong>&nbsp;icon to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Edit Title</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Edit Description</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Star it as a Resource</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Set Permission for Students to download or print the media object</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Edit Keyword Tags</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Once done, click the&nbsp;<strong>Save</strong> icon.</p>

</li>
</ul>
