<p>To star rubrics, click on the <strong>Drawer Settings</strong> icon followed
by the <strong>Star </strong>icon on the rubrics card.</p>

<p></p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may manage the starred rubrics under the <a href="https://www.learning.moe.edu.sg/student-user-guide/organise/star-resources/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Starred Resources</a> folder.</p>

<h2>Add and Edit Rubrics (Enhanced)</h2>
<hr>
<p>Rubrics can be added to Free-Response or Audio-Response Questions to assess
students holistically on performance tasks.</p>
<p>There are two ways teachers can add or edit rubrics:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Use Existing Rubrics</strong> – Add rubrics from your Library or
Starred Resources (available from Dec 2025).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Generate Rubrics using Authoring Copilot (ACP)</strong> – Create
a new set of rubrics or generate a first draft using Authoring Copilot.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h4><strong>Use Existing Rubrics</strong></h4>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>
From a Free-Response or Audio-Response Question, click <strong>Add Rubrics</strong> dropdown
list.
<p><u>Note</u>: From Dec 2025, teachers can also add rubrics from Library
or Starred Resources.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>A maximum of 8 bands (columns) and 6 criteria (rows) can be added in a
set of <strong>Rubrics</strong>. Click the <strong>Overflow</strong> (<strong>3-dot</strong> icon)
to <strong>Add Band</strong>, <strong>Add Criterion</strong>, <strong>Delete Band,</strong> or <strong>Delete Criterion</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
Enter the following fields:
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>Title
</li>
<li>
Band Descriptor
</li>
<li>
Criterion
</li>
<li>Mark Range
<p><u>Note</u>: Please see below on how to use the marks of the rubrics for
the question.</p>
</li><li>
Band Description
<p><u>Note</u>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
You may enter descriptions in the form of text, mathematical formulae,
chemical formulae, and media.
</li>
<li>
From June 2025 Update, teachers can edit the band descriptions after students
have submitted their responses.<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 60%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Add Rubrics to Audio- and Free-Response Questions" src="/images/2Teacher/AU-AddRubrics1.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click outside the Component to save.</p>
</li>
<li>
To add rubric notes, click on the <strong>Drawer Settings</strong> icon
followed by the <strong>Settings</strong> icon on the rubrics cards.
<p><u>Note</u>: Teachers can also add keywords tags and subject/level/content
	map tags.</p></li><p></p>

<li>
<p>When rubrics are added to a quiz, teacher can select questions to be associated
with the rubrics.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Hide_rubrics.jpg">
</div>
</li>
</ul></li></ol>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>Generate Using Authoring Copilot (ACP)</strong></h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Teachers can also use <strong>Authoring Copilot</strong> (ACP) to generate
rubrics by selecting <strong>Generate Rubric (Beta)</strong>.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 60%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/image__12_.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>When you select <strong>Generate Rubric (Beta)</strong>, the <strong>Generate Rubric </strong>page
opens. Here, you can enter short text-based prompts to guide ACP in creating
a rubric from scratch or based on an existing one.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/ALS_The_Future_of_Personalized_Learning__Crafted_Just_for_You___3_.png">
</div>
<p>In the <strong>Instructions to Authoring Copilot</strong> field, type clear
guidance such as:</p>
<p><em>“Create a rubric to assess students’ reasoning with 4 criteria and 3 bands. Criteria should include accuracy, clarity, use of evidence, and structure.”</em>
</p>
<p>After you click <strong>Create</strong>, ACP will generate the rubric automatically
and attach it to the question.</p>
<p>You can review and modify <strong>criteria</strong>, <strong>band descriptors</strong>,
and <strong>mark ranges</strong> as needed.</p>
<p><u>Note</u>:
</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
Rubrics generated via ACP can be adjusted after creation.<p></p>
</li>
<li>
Teachers can refine or overwrite the generated rubric anytime before saving.<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Alternatively, teachers can use ACP to adapt existing rubrics in a <strong>Module</strong>,
the <strong>Library</strong> or <strong>Starred Resources</strong>. To do
so, they can clear fields (criteria, band descriptors and band descriptions)
and click <strong>Populate Rubric (Beta)</strong>.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 60%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/image__13_.png">
</div>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Rubrics Display Settings (Enhanced)</h2>
<hr>
<p>Teachers can edit the rubrics display settings in two ways:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Show/Hide Rubrics before Student Attempt</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Show/Hide Marks for Rubrics</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h4><strong>Show/Hide Rubrics before Student Attempt</strong></h4>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Teachers can check the box <strong>Hide rubrics before attempt </strong>to
hide the rubrics from students during their attempt. The relevant bands
from the rubrics will be shown to students after the question has been
marked.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Hide_rubric.jpg">
</div>
<p><u>Note</u>: This is available for both standalone Free-Response and Audio-Response
Questions, and all quiz modes.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>When using SAFA at quiz level, teacher can also check the box <strong>Hide rubrics before attempt</strong> to
show only the relevant bands from the rubrics, after SAFA has generated
feedback and suggested marks for Free-Response Questions.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h4><strong>Show/Hide Marks for Rubrics</strong></h4>
<ol>
<li>
<p>There could be instances where teachers may not wish to show the marks
tied to the rubric band descriptor, to allow students to focus on the qualitative
feedback provided. In such situations, you can uncheck the box for <strong>Show and use rubrics marks</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>However by default, the <strong>Show and use rubric marks </strong>box
is checked. The total marks will be calculated based on the marks allocated
by the teacher for each criterion in the rubrics. This will also impact
students' quiz scores in a&nbsp;Teacher-Marked Quiz.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Add Rubrics to Audio- and Free-Response Questions" src="/images/2Teacher/AU_ShowUseRubrics.png">
</div>
<p><u>Note</u>: This is only available for Free-Response and Audio-Response
Questions in quiz mode.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teacher can also select <strong>Hide quiz marks </strong>where only the
relevant bands from the rubrics will be shown to students after the questions
have been marked. This can be used for creating personality tests, performance
tasks etc., for students to focus on the feedback.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/hide_quiz_marks.jpg">
</div>
<p><u>Note</u>: This is available for all quiz modes.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ol>
