<h1>(A2,1) Monitor Students' Responses in an Assignment (Enhanced)</h1>
<hr>
<h2>Monitor an Assignment</h2>
<hr>
<p>You can monitor your students' Assignment progress on the Monitor Assignment
page via three ways:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From the Assignment card, click the <strong>Monitoring</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Monitor.svg">
</div>
<p>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From within the Assignment, click the <strong>Monitoring</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Monitor.svg">
</div>
<p>in the header.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From the <strong>Module Plan</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/courseplan32.svg">
</div>
<p>, click the <strong>Monitoring</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Monitor.svg">
</div>
<p>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Note: You can click <strong>Refresh</strong> at the top left corner of the
Monitor Assignment page to view the most up-to-date student submissions.</p>
<h2>Monitor Assignment page (Enhanced)</h2>
<hr>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Monitor Students' Responses in an Assignment" src="/images/2Teacher/As_MonitorStudents.png">
</div>
<p>The following information will be displayed on the Monitor Assignment
page:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Assignment Status</strong>: based on the overall Completion Status
of students assigned.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Download button</strong>: You may choose to download the students’
marks or responses as a CSV file. You will be able to see their individual
and aggregated marks or responses for each questions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><u>Note</u>:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To download students’ response for each question, click on the question
number to view the aggregated students’ responses for that particular question.
From there, click <strong>Download Responses</strong> below the question
stem.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Marks generated by Learning Feedback Assistants will be included in the
total marks as well.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can download Assignment Marks and Response .csv files from Assignment
listing.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Filter</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Filter24.svg">
</div>
<p>: To filter by Student's Assignment, Section and Activity completion status.
Click the <strong>Filter</strong> icon to open the Activities in the Section.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Toggle on/off to show marks under Setting</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Settings24.svg">
</div>
<p>: To show/hide marks on the Heatmap.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Legend</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Legend.svg">
</div>
<p>: Legend for the colours and icons used in the Heatmap.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Heatmap</strong>: Table of students and their response data. All
questions will be indicated in this table. Click the <strong>Section</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Section.svg">
</div>
<p>to navigate to a Section. Within a Section, click the <strong>Activity</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/ActivityIcon.svg">
</div>
<p>,</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/ActivityOptional32.svg">
</div>
<p>or</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Quiz.svg">
</div>
<p>to navigate to an Activity. Team Quizzes will be indicated with the <strong>Team</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/TeamSubmission.svg">
</div>
<p>. Alternatively, click the <strong>Filter</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/FilterTo.svg">
</div>
<p>beside a Section or Activity icon to filter a Section or an Activity.
Within an Activity, click the <strong>Question Number</strong> in the table
header to view the aggregated students' responses for that particular question.</p>
<p><u>Note</u>:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From March 2025 Update, you can view student responses by clicking View
Responses or the visual indicator under the component on the heatmap.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Clicking the Question Number on the Heatmap will redirect you to the Aggregated
Student Responses.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To exit the filtered view, click the <strong>X</strong> beside the <strong>Filter</strong> icon.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Drawings, files, audio clips submitted to an Interactive Thinking Tool
will not be downloaded.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can view section start and due dates when filtering by sections on
the heatmap. You can also click on the dates to access the Assignment Settings
page and adjust the due dates.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h2>Monitor Student Draft Responses</h2>
<hr>
<p>You can monitor your students’ draft responses in the following question
types:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><a href="/teacher-user-guide/author/add-multiple-choice-questions/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Multiple-Choice Questions</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/teacher-user-guide/author/add-fill-in-the-blanks-questions/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Fill-in-the-Blank Questions</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/teacher-user-guide/author/add-click-and-drop-questions/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Click-and-Drop Questions</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/teacher-user-guide/author/add-error-editing-questions/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Error-Editing Questions</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/teacher-user-guide/author/add-free-response-questions/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Free-Response Questions</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/teacher-user-guide/author/add-audio-response-questions/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Audio-Response Questions</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/teacher-user-guide/author/add-multi-part-questions/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Multi-Part Questions Questions</a>
</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>To view your students’ responses:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>On the&nbsp;<strong>Monitor Assignment</strong>&nbsp;page, select the
student's name.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From the Activity List on the left, select the Activity that contains
the response(s).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You will be able to see the draft answer.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><u>Note</u>:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>You cannot edit your students’ draft responses.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If the responses are in a quiz, you can submit them on your students’
behalf.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For draft answers, you will be able to see the name of the student or
teacher who saved the draft and the time it was saved.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For submitted and unsubmitted answers, you will be able to see the name
of the student or teacher who submitted/unsubmitted the answer and the
time it was submitted/unsubmitted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2>Monitor an Interactive Thinking Tool</h2>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Under the Interactive Thinking Tool, click <strong>View Class Submissions</strong> to
open the Interaction Board.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For Interactive Thinking Tool, you can view responses for individual column
or all the columns at once by choosing the <strong>View All</strong> tab.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can click <strong>Refresh</strong> at the top left corner of the interaction
board to view the most up-to-date student submissions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Download the responses from the Interactive Thinking Tool by clicking <strong>Download Responses</strong> under
either of the columns.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The number of Comments is indicated beside the <strong>Comment</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Comment.svg">
</div>
<p>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To view comments for any response, click the Response card.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All Comments made by you and your students will be made visible, arranged
according to the time stamp, with the most recent post at the bottom. Changes
to the state of the comments (e.g. deleted or edited) are also reflected
in the Comments.</p>
<p><u>Note</u>: Names of students who have left a comment cannot be hidden.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To comment on a response, click the <strong>Add</strong> on the bottom right
of the Response card.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>After typing in the comment, click <strong>Post</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can bookmark an Interactive Thinking Tool card by clicking the <strong>Bookmark</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Bookmark.svg">
</div>
<p>. This will allow you to filter the cards in the Interaction Board. Bookmarked
cards will also be displayed first.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Monitor a Poll</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Under the Poll, click <strong>View Class Submissions</strong> to open the
Interaction Board.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The results of the Poll will be displayed as a bar graph. Hovering over
the individual bar graph will show the number of votes for a particular
option.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can also view by individual student’s response by clicking the <strong>List</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/List.svg">
</div>
<p>next to View by.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The legend for the Poll is found below the bar chart.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Monitor a Discussion</h2>
<hr>
<p><u>Note: </u>From March 2025 Update, you may monitor students' submission
status under the component in the heatmap.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Monitor Students' Responses in an Assignment" src="/images/2Teacher/As_Heatmap5.png">
</div>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Under the Discussion, click <strong>View Class Submissions</strong> to open
the Interaction Board.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may click <strong>View Class Participation</strong> to monitor the number
of posts submitted by each student.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can click Refresh at the top left corner of the interaction board
to view the most up-to-date student submissions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Download the responses from the Discussion by clicking <strong>Download Responses</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may also search for posts using the search bar above the posts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can respond to students’ responses by adding a post or adding comments
to their posts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The number of Comments is indicated beside the <strong>Comment</strong> icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Comment.svg">
</div>
<p>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To view comments for any response, click the Response card.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All comments made by you and your students will be made visible, and arranged
according to the time stamp, with the most recent post at the bottom. Changes
to the state of the comments (e.g. deleted or edited) are also reflected
in the comments.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To comment on a response, click <strong>Add</strong> on the bottom right
of the Response card.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>After typing in the comment, click <strong>Post</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><u>Note</u>: Each student can submit more than one post in a Discussion
and teachers can create new posts as well.</p>
