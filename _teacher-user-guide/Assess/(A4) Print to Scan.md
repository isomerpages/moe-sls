<h1>(A4) Print-to-Scan</h1>
<hr>
<p>From March 2025, students’ handwritten mathematical workings can be evaluated
using FA-Math when response sheets are submitted using Print-To-Scan.</p>
<p>Students may complete the quiz on response sheets which are then scanned
and uploaded to SLS, where their responses on hardcopy worksheets can be
transferred to the corresponding questions in SLS for teacher marking.
Marking can also be done with <a href="/teacher-user-guide/assess/add-short-answer-feedback-assistant/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Short Answer Feedback Assistant</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Important</strong>:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Only Multiple-Choice, Multiple-Response and Free-Response questions are
supported.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students should be instructed to tick within the boxes in pencil in order
for the scan to be read correctly.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Changes in answers should be fully erased before submitting.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Using tables in pre-populated answers for Free-Response questions is discouraged.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Teachers can print Teacher-Marked Quizzes as physical worksheets.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<h2>View as Print-to-Scan Quiz</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enter the Module View page of an Assignment.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Open the right menu and click <strong>View as Print-to-Scan Quiz</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The View as Print-to-Scan Quiz subpage will appear</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select a <a href="/teacher-user-guide/assess/mark-teacher-marked-quizzes/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Teacher-Marked Quiz</a> that
is part of your assignment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click on <strong>View Question Paper</strong> or <strong>View Response Sheets</strong> and
your selection will be shown in a new tab.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may save or print a copy of the Question Paper or the Resource Sheets
from the new tab.</p>
</li>
</ol>

<h2>Question Paper Settings</h2>
<hr>
<p>You may customise your Question Paper by adding an image (e.g. your school
logo) or a header description text.</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From the View as Print-to-Scan Quiz subpage, click on <strong>View Question Paper</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the new tab, click on the Settings icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Settings24.svg">
</div>
<p>on the right side.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>On the Question Paper Settings subpage, you can add a logo and/or description
that will appear at the top of the first page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Save</strong>, followed by <strong>X</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 80%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Print-to-Scan" src="/images/2Teacher/AS_Printtoscan2.png">

<h2>Response Sheets Settings</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From the View as Print-to-Scan Quiz subpage, click on <strong>View Response Sheets</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In the new tab, click on the Settings icon</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Settings24.svg">
</div>
<p>on the right side.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>On the Response Sheets Settings subpage, you can do the following:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Select Students to view their personalised response sheets: Click on the
dropdown bar to select a student by Subgroup or Form Class. Select the
subgroup you wish to add by selecting the checkbox beside each subgroup.
To view the list of students within a subgroup, click the <strong>dropdown arrow</strong>.
After selecting the students, their response sheets will appear on the
main screen.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Free-Response Question Settings: Clicking the checkbox “Include writing
lines” to create writing lines for each Free-Response Question.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Save</strong>, followed by <strong>X</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>

<h2>Scanning Quiz Response Sheets</h2>
<hr>
<p><strong>Important</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>You are encouraged to scan files in bulk using photocopiers or scanners.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Each sheet should be saved as an image file (e.g. jpeg, jpg or png). Do
not use PDF.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The uploaded image files must be zipped in a folder (.zip) in order for
it to be processed.</p>
</li>

<h2>Upload Response Sheets</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From the Class Group page where you have your assignment, click on <strong>Upload Response Sheets</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select a zip file with all the scanned worksheets inside and click <strong>Upload</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The results will be processed in approximately 5 minutes and appear under
your notifications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may also click on the Monitoring icon&nbsp;</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:1.5rem; display: inline;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/Icons/Monitor.svg">
</div>
<p>on the Assignment Card to view the student responses.</p>
</li>
</ol>
