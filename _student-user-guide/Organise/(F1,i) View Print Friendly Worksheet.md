<h1>(F1,i) View Print-Friendly Worksheet</h1>
<hr>
<p>Print-friendly Worksheets allow for resources to be downloaded and printed.
Resources from MOE Library, Class Group Resources, and Assignments can
be viewed as Print-Friendly worksheets.</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enter the Module View page of a Module.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Open the right menu and click <strong>View as Print-Friendly Worksheet</strong> (if
permission has been granted).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The print-friendly view will be shown in a new tab.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You may save or print a copy of the Resource from this page.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Note:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Student responses, Notes, Annotations, and Teacher Feedback will not be
included in the Print-Friendly Worksheet view.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From Dec 2025 Update, students can print their completed assignments or
modules, including their responses, suggested answers, feedback and rubrics.
Once the module is completed, a print-friendly version with responses and
answers will be available for printing.</p>
