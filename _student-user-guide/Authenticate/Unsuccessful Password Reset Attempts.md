<h3>Unsuccessful Password Reset Attempts</h3>
<h4>Incorrect Answers to Security Questions</h4>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>
<p>If you answer any of the questions incorrectly, you will be brought to
the Password Reset Unsuccessful page.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/4Troubleshooting/LT-UnsuccesfulPassword.png">
</div>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Retry</strong> to try again.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>If you answer all the questions correctly, you will be brought to the
Reset Password page.</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enter a new password.</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>You will need to enter the new password twice to confirm that you have
entered it correctly.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Submit</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If your password has been successfully reset, you will be brought to the
login page.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<h4>Get more Help</h4>
<hr>
<p>If you still encounter problems resetting your password:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Contact your subject teacher for help.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Alternatively, please contact your <a href="/login-troubleshooting/get-help/get-help-from-your-school/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">School-Based Helpline</a> for
help.</p>
</li>
<li>

6702 6513.</p>
</li>
</ol>
