<h1>(A2,6) Attempt a Free-Response Question</h1>
<hr>
<h2>Free-Response Question</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Your teacher may have:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>attached a pre-populated Google File to a Free-Response Question. Click
<a href="/student-user-guide/assign/attempt-google-attached-files/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>on how to attempt such questions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>turned on Feedback Assistant - Mathematics. Click <a href="/student-user-guide/assess/about-mathematics-feedback-assistant/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> on
how to attempt such questions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>turned on Annotated Feedback Assistant. Click <a href="/student-user-guide/assess/about-annotated-feedback-assistant/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> on
how to attempt such questions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>turned on Short Answer Feedback Assistant. Click <a href="/student-user-guide/assess/about-short-answer-feedback-assistant" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> on
how to attempt such questions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>turned on Interactive Response. Marks will be automatically awarded after
you have interacted with the HTML5 content in the questions.</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>For Free-Response Questions, your may include in your response:</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>text in English, Malay, <a href="/student-user-guide/assess/insert-chinese-or-tamil-text/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Chinese or Tamil</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/student-user-guide/assess/insert-tables/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">tables</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/student-user-guide/assess/insert-mathematical-or-chemical-equations/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">mathematical or chemical equations</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/student-user-guide/assess/insert-emoticon/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">emoticons</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/student-user-guide/assess/insert-drawing/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">drawings</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/student-user-guide/assess/upload-file/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">files</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/student-user-guide/assess/insert-and-edit-links/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">links</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><a href="/student-user-guide/assess/insert-tooltip/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">tooltips</a>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li>
<p>To enlarge your text box, click the <strong>Expand</strong> icon found on
the bottom right of the text box.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/1Student/As-FRQ.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>Some Free-Response Questions may have part of the answer or drawing in
the text box (e.g. Drawing a graph). This is given by your teacher to help
you in refining your answer response.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p><u>Note</u>:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>When you are typing your response, the auto-save indicator will reflect <s>2</s> 1
of the 3 statuses: (1) Autosaving as draft, (2) Saved as draft, or (3)
Autosaving failed. You may also manually click on <strong>Save as Draft</strong>.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/1Student/FreeResponse_Draft.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>If internet connection is unstable, please <strong><u>do not close</u></strong> the
browser page. Your last saved response in SLS will be restored when connectivity
resumes, even if the page is refreshed.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For draft answers, you will be able to see the name of the student or
teacher who saved the draft and the time it was saved.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For submitted and unsubmitted answers, you will be able to see the name
of the student or teacher who submitted/unsubmitted the answer and the
time it was submitted/unsubmitted.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If your teacher has set a recommended time, you will be able to see it
below the Question number.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h2>Rubrics in Free-Response Questions</h2>
<hr>
<p>Some Free-Response Questions contain <strong>Rubrics</strong> for reference.
Click <a href="/student-user-guide/assess/attempt-a-question-with-rubrics/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> to
find out more.</p>
<h2>Submitting a Free-Response Question</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>In a standalone question that is not part of a quiz, you can submit your
response by clicking the <strong>Submit</strong> button. You can also choose <strong>Save as Draft</strong> if
you would like to revisit this question again after you have visited other
pages or logged out. Your response will be saved.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>In an Auto-Graded Quiz and a Teacher-Marked Quiz, you can only submit
your response after attempting the last question.</p>
</li>
</ol>
