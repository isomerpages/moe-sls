<h1>(A3,11) Speech Evaluation Tool (Enhanced)</h1>
<hr>
<p><u>Note</u>:
<br>Speech Evaluation is available in English, Chinese, Malay and Tamil.</p>
<h3>Speech Evaluation</h3>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>If Speech Evaluation Tool has been enabled by your teacher, you may record
your audio clip for submission to the Speech Evaluator tool.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can record multiple audio files. However only 1 audio file can be
submitted or saved as a draft.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Speech Evaluation" src="/images/1Student/As-SE.png">
</div>
<h3>View Speech Evaluation Feedback</h3>
<hr>
<p>The Speech Evaluation Tool will generate a transcript of the submitted
audio recording. The transcript will be automatically annotated for errors
such as mispronunciation, inserts and omissions for English, Chinese and
Tamil Language. Support for Malay Language will be implemented in future
updates.</p>
<p>Note:
<br>You can play the correct audio for mispronunciation or omission errors
by clicking on the respective words in the transcript</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Speech Evaluation" src="/images/2Teacher/AU_SpeechEvaluate5.png">
</div>
<p>You will see the marks awarded by the Speech Evaluation tool based on
the bands and mark range allocated.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Speech Evaluation" src="/images/2Teacher/AU_SpeechEvaluate6.png">
</div>

</div>

Words Correct Per Minute will be displayed.</p>
