<h1>(D3) About Short Answer Feedback Assistant (ShortAnsFA)</h1>
<p>The Short Answer Feedback Assistant (ShortAnsFA) provides auto-generated content-related feedback and suggested marks for close-ended, short answer questions for any subject and level.</p>
<ol>
	<li>Your teacher may add the tool in a Free-Response Question as part of a Teacher-Marked Quiz.</li>
	<li>Once the quiz has been completed, you may submit your response to the question, by clicking <strong>Submit Quiz</strong>.</li>
