<h1>(B3) View and Respond to Teacher Comments</h1>
<hr>
<h2>View Teacher Comments</h2>
<hr>
<p>Your teacher may leave Comments on your responses to Free-Response, File
Submission, and Audio-Response Questions.</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Open the Assignment and navigate to the Free-Response, File Submission,
or Audio-Response Question.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the <strong>Teacher Comments</strong> tab to view the comments given
by your teacher. To view the comments left by your teacher for a Google-attached
File, click <a href="/student-user-guide/assign/attempt-google-attached-files/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 50%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="View and Respond to Teacher Comments" src="/images/1Student/As-TeacherComments.png">
</div>
<p><u>Note</u>: If enabled by your teacher, you will receive a notification
that feedback has been given on your Assignment.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 50%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="View and Respond to Teacher Comments" src="/images/1Student/As-TeacherComments1.png">
</div>
<h2>Respond to Teacher Comments (Enhanced)</h2>
<hr>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Open the Assignment and navigate to the Free-Response, File Submission,
or Audio-Response Question.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select the <strong>Teacher Comments</strong> tab to view the feedback given
by your teacher.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Add</strong> to bring out the text box. Type your response,
insert a drawing, attach a file, add file from Google or upload an audio
recording, then click <strong>Post</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>You can also mention your teacher(s) in the comment by typing '@' and
selecting their names from the dropdown list. Your teacher(s) will be notified.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click <strong>Overflow</strong> (<strong>3-dot</strong> icon) to <strong>Edit</strong> or <strong>Delete</strong> your
comment.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 50%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="View and Respond to Teacher Comments" src="/images/1Student/As-TeacherComments2.png">
</div>
<p><u>Note</u>: If your teacher has provided feedback using the Drawing Tool,
you may click Reply to respond to your teacher’s comments by annotating
on the same image. You may also download the image (png) for future reference.</p>
