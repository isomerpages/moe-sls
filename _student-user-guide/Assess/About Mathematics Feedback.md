<h1>About Mathematics Feedback</h1>
<p>A mathematics evaluation engine that offers step-by-step feedback to students on their input.</p>
<ol>
<li>Your teacher may add the tool as a Prepopulated Student Answer space in a Free-Response Question. You will then be informed on the feedback provided for the question. </li>
<img style="width: 100%;" src="images/1Student/AS-AboutMathematicsFeedback01.png">
<li>If enabled, you will be able to access hints when attempting the question.</li>
<img style="width: 100%;" src="images/1Student/AS-AboutMathematicsFeedback02.png">
<li>If enabled, you may also click <strong>Check</strong> to review their work and receive immediate feedback.</li>
<img style="width: 100%;" src="images/1Student/AS-AboutMathematicsFeedback03.png">
<li>Once the work has been completed, you may submit your response to the question, by clicking <strong>Submit</strong>.</li>
<img style="width: 100%;" src="images/1Student/AS-AboutMathematicsFeedback04.png">
</ol>
