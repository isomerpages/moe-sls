<h2>Search for Self-Study Resources</h2>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>From the <strong>MOE Library</strong> homepage, click on the <strong>Search Bar</strong> at
the top of the page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Enter your search term(s) in the Search Bar. You may search by resource
title, subject, level, content map, or keywords.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Press <strong>Enter</strong> to view the Search Results page.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Browse available resources and review the resource cards to find modules
relevant to your learning needs, like reading the module description, subject
tags, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use the available filters, such as <strong>Level</strong> and <strong>Subject</strong>,
to refine your search results.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Select filters as needed. The results count will refresh automatically
in real time.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use the <strong>Sort by</strong> dropdown to organise resources based on
your preferred search option, such as Relevance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Browse available resources and review the resource cards to find modules
relevant to your learning needs. Click on a resource card to view more
details, such as the title, description, subject, level, and tags.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the <strong>Expand icon</strong> to open a resource in a new tab.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Use the <strong>Overflow (3-dot) icon</strong> for additional actions, where
available.</p>
</li>
</ol>
