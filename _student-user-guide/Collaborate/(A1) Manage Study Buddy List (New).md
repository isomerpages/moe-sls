<h1>(A1) Manage Study Buddy List (New)</h1>
<hr>
<p>From the Dec 2025 Update, students can maintain a <strong>Study Buddy List</strong> which
automatically shares shareable assignments enabled by teachers, so that
students can see each other’s work for revision, peer review, sharing of
assignment notes and answers.</p>
<h3>Access Study Buddy List</h3>
<hr>
<ol>
<li>
<p>To access the study buddy list, open the right menu and click <strong>My Study Buddy List</strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>From here, you can either:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>add Study Buddies by clicking on <strong>Add Study Buddies</strong>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>review pending study buddy requests by clicking on <strong>Accept/Reject</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Frame_8__1_.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>remove study buddies by selecting the checkbox(es) beside the name followed
by <strong>Remove</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<h3>Add Study Buddies</h3>
<hr>
<p>You may add up to 100 study buddies, including pending requests.</p>
<p>Students can add <strong>Study Buddies</strong> from:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>Study Buddy List</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Assignment page</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Enter an assignment with <a href="https://www.learning.moe.edu.sg/student-user-guide/collaborate/about-assignment-sharing/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">Assignment Sharing</a> enabled.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Click the&nbsp;<strong>User Avatar</strong> icon followed by the <strong>Plus</strong> (<strong>+</strong> icon).</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 50%;" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Untitled__1_.png">
</div>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Use the search bar to search for a study buddy. Click <strong>Send Request</strong> to
select the study buddy that you want to share your assignment with.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Frame_7.png">
</div>
<p>Note: You are not allowed to withdraw the sharing request after it has
been sent.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The status will change from&nbsp;”Send Request”&nbsp;to&nbsp;”Request
Sent”. This means that you are waiting for your classmate to accept your
request.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
</li>
</ol>
