<h1>(A2) Interact in a Shared Assignment</h1>
<hr>
<p>In a shared assignment, you can view and share Notes with your study buddies
and learn from one another. Assignment Sharing will work for both you and
your peers and cannot be cancelled.</p>
<ol>
<li>
<p>To view a Shared Assignment, click the <strong>User Avatar</strong> icon
followed by the name of your peer.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>When you are viewing your peer's assignment, your User Avatar will show
the Viewer Avatar icon.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="Interact in a Shared Assignment" src="/images/1Student/CO-InteractShared1.png">
</div>
</li>
<li>
<p>If commenting is turned on, you will be able to comment on any Notes or
Annotations cards in your peer's Assignment and vice versa. You can also
@-mention your study buddy and they will receive notifications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>As the owner of your Assignment, you are able to turn commenting on or
off (by default, commenting is turned off).
</p><p><u>Note</u>:
</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
Teachers have the authority to turn commenting on or off.
</li>
<li>
Teachers are able to delete any comments.<p></p>
</li>
</ul>

</li><li>
<p>To turn commenting on or off, open <strong>Overflow</strong> (<strong>3-dot</strong> icon)
found on the top right of the Annotation card and click <strong>Comment On / Off</strong>.</p>
</li>
</ol>
